

# Details: 
  
Track the amount of money spent in lunch daily by the team.

# Actions:

## New Budget:

Mr. Piccolo has given a new lunch budget this month.

## New Expense:

Whenever a team member orders food.

  - Month

  - Date

## Expense

  - Amount

  - User

  - Date

  - Restaurant

  - Receipt
